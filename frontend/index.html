FAQs

What is QuickServe?

QuickServe is an AI-powered platform that simplifies restaurant ordering and clinic appointment booking. It uses a single chatbot interface to handle both tasks seamlessly. Users can interact with the chatbot to place orders or schedule appointments.

How does it work?

Simply type your request into the chatbot, whether it's for ordering food or booking a clinic appointment. The chatbot will guide you through the process and provide options based on your needs. It's designed to be intuitive and user-friendly.

Is it secure?

Yes, QuickServe prioritizes user security and privacy. All personal information is encrypted and stored securely. We adhere to industry standards to protect your data.

Can I create an account?

Absolutely! Creating an account allows you to access personalized features and save your preferences. You can easily register through the QuickServe interface.
